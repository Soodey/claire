Meet Claire

Dedicated to understanding and improving the lives of dogs and their people since 2015.

My journey began as a dog walker and evolved through diverse roles in daycares and veterinary settings. These experiences gave me a deep appreciation for canine behavior, health, and communication.

Over the years, I’ve worked in multiple Chicago dog daycares in a variety of roles—including receptionist, handler, trainer, and medicator—as well as in a veterinary setting. I am dedicated to continued education and love that I get to constantly be learning. I've had the privilege of studying under respected industry leaders, and I bring that knowledge to every training session I lead.
