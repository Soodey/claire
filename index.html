About Claire

Claire Horvat (she/her/hers) is a dedicated dog trainer and behavior consultant with experience working at Pet Harmony. Claire's passion lies in creating communication and understanding between furry friends and their people. She believes that recognizing what your animal is trying to tell you is the key to nurturing a more confident pet and achieving a harmonious lifestyle for everyone.

Claire's journey began in the world of theatre, where she learned the art of performance and storytelling. Yet, she soon discovered that something was missing. That missing element turned out to be furry critters. She started her career working in doggy daycares around Chicago and later apprenticed under an experienced dog trainer. During this time, she immersed herself in science-based, positive reinforcement training and behavior modification.

Eventually, Claire ventured into behavior consulting, driven by her fascination with the bond between pets and their people. She loves devising personalized plans that ensure every family member’s needs are met. Claire is also a proud member of the IAABC, is Fear Free Certified, and holds the designation of Certified Professional Dog Trainer – Knowledge Assessed.
