Meet Your Trainer, Claire

My journey in the dog world began in 2015 as a dog walker. Since then, I’ve dedicated my career to understanding and improving the lives of dogs and their people.

Over the years, I’ve worked in multiple Chicago dog daycares in a variety of roles—including receptionist, handler, trainer, and medicator—as well as in a veterinary setting. These diverse experiences have given me a deep appreciation for canine behavior, health, and communication, and inspired my commitment to helping dogs thrive.

I am dedicated to continued education and love that I get to constantly be learning. I've had the privilege of studying under respected industry leaders, and I bring that knowledge to every training session I lead.
