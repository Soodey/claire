About Claire Horvat

Claire Horvat (she/her/hers) is a dedicated dog trainer and behavior consultant with a passion for creating clear communication and deep understanding between people and their pets. She believes that recognizing what your animal is trying to tell you is the key to nurturing a more confident pet and achieving a harmonious lifestyle for everyone.

Claire's journey began in theatre but her love for animals led her to doggy daycares and eventually an apprenticeship under an experienced trainer. Immersing herself in science-based, positive reinforcement training, she developed a fascination with the unique bond between pets and their people.

As a proud member of the IAABC, Fear Free Certified, and a Certified Professional Dog Trainer – Knowledge Assessed (CPDT-KA), Claire loves devising personalized plans that ensure every family member’s needs are met.
